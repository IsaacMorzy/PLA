PeaceLeague Africa’s latest content and UX updates focus on one outcome: helping people decide to support faster, with better confidence.

## What changed

### 1) Campaign information is easier to evaluate
Campaign pages now prioritize:
- need clarity,
- progress visibility,
- and action readiness.

### 2) Trust language is more specific
We replaced generic claims with concrete guidance around:
- how donors evaluate legitimacy,
- what creators should communicate,
- and where to find proof signals before donating.

### 3) Conversion paths are clearer
Core donation and launch flows now use consistent action language and cleaner supporting copy.

## Why this matters
Donors should not need to decode campaign quality.
Creators should not need guesswork to present credibility.

Better structure improves both sides of the marketplace.

## What’s next
We’ll continue publishing:
- donor decision frameworks,
- creator launch playbooks,
- and campaign spotlight updates with measurable progress context.
