# LinkedIn Post #1 — Donor Trust (Awareness → Consideration)

## Objective
Drive qualified traffic to `/campaigns` by addressing the core donation blocker: lack of verifiable clarity.

## Final Copy
Most people don’t avoid giving because they don’t care.
They avoid giving when they can’t verify what happens next.

At PeaceLeague Africa, campaign pages are structured so donors can check three things fast:
1) What the need is
2) How close the campaign is to its goal
3) Whether progress is visible before they contribute

When decision signals are clear, support moves faster.

If you’re evaluating where to give this week, start with campaigns that show context and momentum clearly.

**Browse campaigns:** https://peaceleague.africa/campaigns

## Creative brief
- Visual: 3-panel card (Need / Progress / Momentum)
- Brand colors: charcoal + gold + terracotta
- CTA in image: “Browse campaigns”
